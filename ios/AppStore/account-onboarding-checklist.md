# Account onboarding checklist

Use this checklist to complete Apple account onboarding before the first App Store release.

## 1) Apple ID and security

- [ ] Create a dedicated Apple ID for publishing (or confirm existing one).
- [ ] Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- [ ] Add a trusted phone number and a backup recovery method.

## 2) Apple Developer Program

- [ ] Enroll into Apple Developer Program: Individual or Organization.
- [ ] Verify legal entity details (for Organization account).
- [ ] Complete payment for annual membership ($99/year).
- [ ] Wait for account activation email.

## 3) App Store Connect access

- [ ] Sign in to App Store Connect.
- [ ] Add team members and roles (Admin, App Manager, Developer) if needed.
- [ ] Accept all pending agreements in Agreements, Tax, and Banking.

## 4) Tax and banking (if monetization planned)

- [ ] Fill tax forms for your country/region.
- [ ] Add bank account details for payouts.
- [ ] Verify legal name and address consistency across Apple forms.

## 5) Internal release readiness

- [ ] Decide app ownership model (single developer vs company account).
- [ ] Document who can submit builds and who can approve release.
- [ ] Store all account credentials in a secure password manager.

## Completion criteria

The account setup stage is complete when:

- Apple Developer membership is active.
- App Store Connect has no pending agreement/tax/banking blockers.
- At least one team member can submit builds from Xcode Organizer.
