Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Univolt Tashkent Explorer ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use our application.

Information we collect

  • Approximate or precise location (only after your permission) to show nearby charging stations.
  • Technical diagnostics such as app version and crash logs (if enabled in your release setup).
  • Favorites stored locally on your device.

How we use information

  • To provide core app features (map, station discovery, favorites).
  • To improve reliability and performance.
  • To support user requests and bug investigation.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers strictly to operate the service.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for service operation and legal obligations. Local data can be removed by uninstalling the app.

Your choices

  • You can disable location access in iOS Settings at any time.
  • You can contact us to request information or deletion where applicable.
